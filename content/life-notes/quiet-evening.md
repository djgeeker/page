## Evening

Some notes do not need to become long essays. They only need enough structure to preserve the mood, the time, and the small observation.

## What I Noticed

- The room became quiet before the sky was fully dark.
- A short paragraph can hold more than a crowded layout.
- The best interface for notes should stay behind the words.

## A Small Rule

Keep life notes light. Let them feel like a calm record, not a dashboard.
